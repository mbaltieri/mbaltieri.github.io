## Talk at Models of Consciousness

Manuel gave a *Showcase* talk on October 4th for the annual meeting of the Association for Mathematical Consciousness Science (AMCS)](https://amcs-community.org), [Models of Consciousness 6](https://amcs-community.org/events/moc6-2025/), held in Sapporo, Japan.
For this talk, Manuel covered some work in progress on a toy model of mathematical phenomenology of Braitenberg vehicles, slides available [here](/assets/pdf/presentations/BraitenbergMoC2025.pdf).
