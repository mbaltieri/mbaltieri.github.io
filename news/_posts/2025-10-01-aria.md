## Awarded a grant by ARIA - Mathematics for Safe AI

Manuel was awarded a grant by the Advanced Research and Innovation Agency ([ARIA](https://aria.org.uk)), in the opportunity space of [Mathematics for Safe AI](https://www.aria.org.uk/opportunity-spaces/mathematics-for-safe-ai#opportunityseeds), with a project titled "[Combining Physical and Intentional Stance for Safe AI](https://www.aria.org.uk/opportunity-spaces/mathematics-for-safe-ai?cardId=combining-physical-intentional-stance-safe-ai#opportunityseeds)".
The project is for two years, 2025-2027.
